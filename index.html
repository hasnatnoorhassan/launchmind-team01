EcoEats: Smarter Dining, Sustainable Choices

Your gateway to reducing food waste and dining on a budget.

Features

Location-Based Food Browsing

Allows customers to browse available meals based on their geographic location and customize the search by price range.

Secure In-App Payment System

Enables a seamless and secure payment process directly within the app, supporting multiple payment methods.

Real-time Availability Updates

Restaurants can update the availability of surplus food items in real-time, ensuring customers have the most current offerings.

Regular Deal Notifications

Sends personalized notifications to users about new and expiring deals based on their location and dietary preferences.

Review System

Collects anonymous reviews and ratings for each completed order, aiding in transparency and quality control without revealing restaurant names.
