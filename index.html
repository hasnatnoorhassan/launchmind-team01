MealSteal

Eat Great, Spend Less, Waste Less

A mobile app for eco-conscious and cost-conscious diners to purchase surplus end-of-day meals from local restaurants at discounted prices.

Features

📍

Geo-Location Based Meal Browsing

Allows users to view available surplus meals based on their current location.

📅

Real-Time Inventory Updates

Enables restaurants to update the availability of surplus food in real time.

🔒

Secure Payment Processing

Provides a secure and seamless payment experience for users directly through the app.

How It Works

1. Search

Find discounted end-of-day meals near you.

2. Select

Choose your favorite surplus meal.

3. Purchase

Complete your purchase securely through the app.
