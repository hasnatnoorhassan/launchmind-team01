Features

Real-Time Meal Updates

Allows restaurants to update available meals in real time as inventory changes.

Seamless Order and Payment Processing

Enables customers to browse, select, and pay for meals directly through the app.

Meal Rating System

Provides customers the ability to rate and review their meal experience after purchase.

Dietary Restriction Filters

Allows users to filter available meals based on their dietary needs such as vegan, gluten-free, etc.

Anonymous Restaurant Listings

Prevents customer bias by hiding restaurant names, focusing only on food details, quantity, and price.
