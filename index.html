Meet SavorySurplus

An innovative mobile app connecting like-minded consumers with delicious surplus meals from local restaurants, all at discounted prices. Perfect for those who value their wallet and the environment.

Features

Real-Time Food Listing

Allows restaurants to list surplus food items as they become available and update in real time.

Location and Price Filtering

Enables users to filter meals based on their current location and desired price range.

Integrated Payment Gateway

Provides users with a seamless and secure payment process within the app.

Anonymous Browsing

Displays only meal details, quantity, and price without revealing restaurant names, encouraging unbiased sustainable choices.

Push Notifications

Notifies users when new deals are available based on their location or preferences.
